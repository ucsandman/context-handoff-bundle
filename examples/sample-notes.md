# Portfolio Synthesis Notes

## Scope
Reviewed multiple projects from the last six months to identify overlap, consolidation opportunities, and new product directions.

## Projects mentioned
- DashClaw
- Practical Systems
- TreasuryClaw
- Budget-Aware Research Agent
- OpenClaw-Pro

## Findings
- DashClaw and Practical Systems overlap in client-facing automation, governance, and operational surfaces.
- The budget-aware research agent is a promising x402 wedge and should remain legible as a standalone capability even if folded into a larger platform.
- TreasuryClaw proved there is value in strong constrained workflows with real artifacts and evaluation.
- Several projects share the same underlying pattern: agent capability plus oversight plus durable artifacts.

## Opportunities
- Merge overlapping governance and operational concepts into DashClaw v3.
- Treat Practical Systems as service delivery and DashClaw as platform substrate.
- Preserve reusable artifacts across sessions to reduce re-research cost.

## Open questions
- Which projects should remain branded separately versus folded inward?
- What is the right boundary between product, consulting layer, and internal operating system?
